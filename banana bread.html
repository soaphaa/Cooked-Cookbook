number two: your salmon !!

optional: skin your salmon

cut your salmon into 1-inch cubes (or larger depending on preffered size)

just remember - size affects cooking time !!

toss the salmon cubes into the sauce, ensuring an even coat

number three: cooking prep !!

grab a medium size pan for 1-2 pieces of salmon

turn your stove onto low-simmer heat

use the circumference of the pan as a guide on how much oil to put

do a circle in the shape of the outline

wait until you can feel a medium amount of heat by hovering your hand over the pan's surface.

number four: cooking the salmon !!

place all the cubes with enough space from the others to rotate

let the salmon cook for 2-3 minutes on one side, then flip and cook for another 2-3 minutes on the other side

of course, 2-3 minutes is a vague suggestion
