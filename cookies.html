COOKIES !!


Serving size: 36 cookies
Prep

Preheat oven to 375 F(190 degrees C). Line three baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.

In a medium bowl mix flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Set aside.

Mixing

Cream together butter and sugars until combined.

Beat in eggs and vanilla until light (about 1 minute).

Mix in the dry ingredients until combined.

Add chocolate chips and mix well.

Baking!

Roll 2-3 Tablespoons of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.

Bake in preheated oven for 8-10 minutes. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.

COMPLETE!!

Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cooling rack. Enjoy!
