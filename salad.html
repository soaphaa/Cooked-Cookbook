Arugula Salad

fresh arugula, crisp apple, goat cheese, and a lemon honey mustard dressing

Ingredients

① make the dressing

in a small bowl, whisk together 3 tbsp olive oil, 1½ tbsp lemon juice, 1 tbsp honey, and 1 tsp dijon mustard

season with salt and pepper to taste

whisk until fully combined and slightly thickened

taste and adjust — more honey for sweetness, more lemon for tang

② prep your toppings

thinly slice the apple

if prepping ahead, toss the apple slices in a little lemon juice to prevent browning

thinly slice the red onion

soak the onion slices in cold water for 5 minutes if you want a milder flavour

crumble the goat cheese into small chunks

③ assemble the salad

add the arugula to a large bowl as your base

scatter the apple slices evenly over the arugula

add the dried cranberries or raisins

layer on the red onion slices

sprinkle the crumbled goat cheese on top
