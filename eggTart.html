Egg Tarts

Full Serving Size: 8
Step 1: The mix!

Combine milk, cream, and sugar together in a saucepan.

Heat the mix over the stove with medium heat, stirring until the sugar is fully dissolved.

Be careful not to overheat - Make sure the mixture does not start boiling!

Once the sugar is dissolved, remove the mixture from heat and leave it to cool for a bit.

In separate bowl, mix the egg yolks and whole eggs and whisk until everything is fully combined

Pour the warm milk & cream mix into the eggs

Be sure to do this slowly and stir continuously to prevent the eggs from clumping!

Pour the entire mixture through a strainer to ensure that large clumps are filtered out.

Step 2: Filling the tart crusts!

Place the tart crusts on a baking tray.

Fill each crust with the mix (step 1) until they are 80-90% full

Step 3: Bake!

Preheat the oven to 395°F

Bake the tarts for 25 minutes!

Optional: Leave the oven on broil mode for approx 2 minutes, so that the top of the filling turns slightly brown/gold.

Step 4: Serve!

Let the freshly baked egg tarts cool slightly before serving.

Enjoy!! Egg tarts can be served both warm and cold.
